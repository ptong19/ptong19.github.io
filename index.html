A Hybrid or an Electric vehicle?

  • Electric
  • Hybrid

Let's compare them!

Hybrid

  • Gasoline-powered internal combustion is the main power source
  • Electric motor is used to complement the combustion engine
  • Electricity is generated on board

Electric

  • The battery-powered electric motor is the main power source
  • The engine is used to complement the electric motor and extend the range
  • Grid-supplied electricity

Vehicle types

Hybrid: Prius

Electric: Tesla
